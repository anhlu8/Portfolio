About me

How it all began

As a person who always want to improve productivity and efficiency, I quickly found my passion in Project Management (PM)/Business Analysis (BA). I started learning PM/BA on my own from differnet online materials to help improve business processes at work. The more I learned about PM/BA, the bigger a fan of Agile Development/Design Thinking I became. I decided to attend Philly Tech Week 2017 to learn more about Agile Project Management and was amazed by what Technology could do.

To me, passion and motivation should go hand-in-hand to create balance. What motivates me in life is the ability to contanstly learn new skills and improve myself, to solve and to create something meaningful and helpful to others. I believe Technology is the answer to my quest!

...and now

I'm currently enrolled in a 24-week full-stack coding bootcamp at UPENN with the hope of getting a full-stack developer position after graduating.

My vision for post-coding-bootcamp program is that I will be able to develop new applications or solve technical problems. At the same time, I will be able to worke and collabore and learn more about Agile development methodologies for Project Management.
